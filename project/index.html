Project Overview

Our AI-Powered Crop Yield Prediction and Optimization system is designed to help farmers make data-driven decisions to maximize their crop yields while optimizing resource usage. By leveraging historical agricultural data, weather patterns, and soil information, our platform provides accurate yield predictions and personalized recommendations.
